About Us

Game Developers

In Sept. 2017, a group of teenagers created a club dedicated to developing applications of all types. From that club, a smaller group decided to collaborate on the creation of a game. About a year later, we have plans to release a demo late 2017.



Completely Student Led

Each part of the game development process, from scripting to modeling, is led by an individual of the team. Skills learned as a member of Vibrant Key include leadership, outreach, communication, scripting, and modeling
